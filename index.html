About us

WITT - What In The Teddy?

RELEASED 20th FEBRUARY!

WITT is a collection of NFT's that features only one avatar, Teddy. This little bear is a friendly and close character to everyone, almost as a reference to ourselves or something that is dear to us. Teddy is constantly in very particular situations. Sometimes it seems that he was part of some stories we already know, other times it seems that he changed those same plots or created new ones.

Unlike most collections, this collection does not have an infinity of characters, but an infinity of moments. Each moment captured is like a story that tells itself or that gives free rein to each person's imagination to create their own plot.

There is no digital programming involved in this collection! All tokens base are hand-drawn, making each piece unique. New tokens will be released frequently, for an undetermined time, always keeping that promised uniqueness! And with the guarantee that we will always use intelligent, subtle and pragmatic humor, whether be used cultural, pop, political or extraterrestrial topics.

Being part of the WITT family is owning a moment that can describe an huge number of situations. You will decide the time, the place, the condition and who was present at the frozen instant of your WITT... BE CREATIVE! Yet only WITT’s owners will be able to buy the special editions that will be released every 50 WITT’s!

All members of the WITT Family can suggest new moments where we can found our friend teddy. All suggestions will be taken into consideration and perhaps many will come true.

We promise to make this collection an opportunity for creativity, good humor and continuous storytelling! All together, we might make the most famous teddy bear on the planet the biggest and most hilarious digital community.
